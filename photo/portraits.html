Summary

Everyone deserves at least one good photo of themselves. While doing portraiture, I work with the subject beforehand to construct a mood board for inspiration of poses, lighting, outfit, and overall style. Using natural lighting and influence from brands such as Anthropologie, Madewell, and Urban Outfitters, I shoot with minimal equipment specifically on a Canon 5D Mark II to create the most natural experience for models. No reflectors, artificial lighting, or assistants needed.
