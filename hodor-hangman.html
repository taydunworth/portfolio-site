Summary

Enter the Seven Kingdoms and test your luck at the gallows with Hodor Hangman. Themed around Game of Thrones, this is a twist on the classic hangman game using words and phrases related to the television series.

This project was created with node.js, DOM manipulation with JavaScript, express, mustache, HTML, and CSS. All illustrations were created in Adobe Illustrator.
