Project

Logo Design

Summary

To rebrand the urban street culture blog Soul Culture, I was commissioned to create a logo design that would be used digitally, in print, and on apparel. The focus of this project was based around creating a logo that embraces the individuality that coincides with street culture. This design combines a custom made font and illustration prepared in Adobe Illustrator.
