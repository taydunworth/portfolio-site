Client

Ironworks Hotels

Project

Website Design and Development

Summary

Ironworks Hotel offers a bespoke experience with its two locations of boutique hotels. To match its unique approach to hospitality, Ironworks requested a website that reached beyond the non-traditional format for a hotel and showcased its industrial-inspired interiors.

To create this website, I designed and provided wireframes before moving on to the development stage using WordPress, custom PHP, HTML, and CSS. This project consisted of a site for the current hotel in Beloit, Wisconsin and a landing page for the upcoming location in Indianapolis, Indiana.
